Meet Bruce

My promise is that you will like working with me. I am open, honest, and will provide the necessary challenges and opportunities to help you and your company meet your organizational goals. I am not a yes-man and if I do not see how I can help, I will refer you to somebody else.

I'm a popular speaker at conferences and industry events. As an entrepreneur, I created the award-winning IdeaJam idea management software. I also authored a course for Microsoft's Lynda.com.

You can also find me teaching web development and design at colleges and universities in Washington State, and serving on several advisory boards for local schools and charities.
